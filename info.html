Mayelana ne-Alfa Online Radio

Ubani futhi yini i-Alfa Online Radio?

I-Radio Alfa yaqala ukusakaza Izindaba Ezinhle ku-FM ngoMeyi ka-2000. I-Alfa Online Radio yaqala ukusakaza nge-inthanethi ngoSepthemba ka-2023 ngesiSwati nangesiZulu. Abalaleli bangalalela izinhlelo nokuqukethwe kwe-Alfa Online Radio kumakhalekhukhwini abo nakumakhompyutha. Uhlelo lokusebenza olusha, oluhlelwe kahle ukusetshenziswa kumakhalekhukhwini, lwethulwa ngoJanuwari ka-2026.

I-Alfa Online Radio isakaza ukufundwa kweBhayibheli ivesi nevesi, ukufundisa iBhayibheli, kanye nokuqukethwe okukhuthazayo nokwakha ukholo. Izinhlelo nezindaba ezihlobene nokusakaza zabelwana ngazo ngeqembu le-WhatsApp Community.

Umsebenzi we-Alfa Online Radio uchazwa kahle kakhulu yileli vesi leBhayibheli: Ngokuba asizishumayeli thina, kodwa sishumayela uKristu Jesu eyiNkosi nokuthi siyizikhonzi zenu ngenxa kaJesu. (2 kwabaseKorinte 4:5 isiZulu 1959)
