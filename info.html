Mayelana ne-Alfa Online Radio

Ngubani futsi yini i-Alfa Online Radio?

IRadio Alfa yacala kusakata Tindzaba Letimnandzi ku-FM ngenyanga yaMeyi ngemnyaka wa-2000. I-Alfa Online Radio yacala kusakateka nge-inthanethi ngenyanga yaSepthemba ngemnyaka wa-2023 ngesiSwati nangesiZulu. Balaleli bangalalela tinhlelo nemisebenti ye-Alfa Online Radio ngemakhalekhukhwini abo kanye nangemakhompyutha. I-app lensha, lehlelelwe kahle kusebenta ngemakhalekhukhwini, yethulwa ngenyanga yaBhimbidvwane (January) ngemnyaka wa-2026.

Umsebenti we-Alfa Online Radio uchazwa kahle kakhulu yilamavesi eBhayibheli lalandzelako: Asitishumayeli tsine, kodvwa sishumayela kutsi Khristu Jesu uyiNkhosi, tsine-ke sitisebenti tenu ngenca yakhe. (2 kubaseKorinthe 4:5)

I-Alfa Online Radio isakata kufundvwa kweBhayibheli ngevesi nangevesi, kufundziswa kweBhayibheli, kanye nemicondvo nekukhutsata lokwakha umoya. Luhlelo lwetinhlelo netindzaba letimayelana nekusakata kwabelwana ngato ngeWhatsApp Community Group.
