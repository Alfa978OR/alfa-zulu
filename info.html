Ubani futhi yini i-Alfa Online Radio?

IRadio Alfa yaqala ukusakaza iVangeli ku-FM ngenyanga kaMeyi ngonyaka ka-2000. I-Alfa Online Radio yaqala ukusakaza ku-inthanethi ngenyanga kaSepthemba ngonyaka ka-2023 ngesiSwati nangesiZulu. Abalaleli bangafinyelela izinhlelo nokuqukethwe kwe-Alfa Online Radio besebenzisa amafoni abo aphathwayo kanye namakhompyutha.

Uhlelo lokusebenza olusha, oluhlelwe kahle ukuze lusebenze kumafoni aphathwayo, lwethulwa ngenyanga kaJanuwari ngonyaka ka-2026.

I-Alfa Online Radio isakaza ukufundwa kweBhayibheli ivesi nevesi, ukufundisa kweBhayibheli, kanye nokuqukethwe okukhuthazayo nokwakha ukholo. Izinhlelo zokusakaza kanye nezindaba ezihlobene zabelwana ngazo ngeQembu Lomphakathi le-WhatsApp.
